# 📖 Overview

Customer shopping behavior provides valuable insights into customer preferences, purchasing habits, and revenue generation. Businesses can use these insights to improve inventory planning, optimize marketing strategies, personalize customer experiences, and increase profitability.

This project performs a complete exploratory data analysis (EDA) on customer shopping data using Python and SQL, followed by an interactive Power BI dashboard for business reporting.

The project demonstrates a complete analytics workflow starting from raw data preprocessing to generating business recommendations.

---

# 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze customer demographics and purchasing behavior.
- Identify high-performing product categories.
- Understand customer spending patterns.
- Analyze seasonal shopping trends.
- Evaluate customer satisfaction through review ratings.
- Study customer purchase frequency.
- Build an interactive business dashboard.
- Generate actionable business insights for decision-making.

# 💡 Key Business Insights

### 👥 Customer Demographics

- Customers belong to multiple age groups, indicating a diverse customer base.
- Gender distribution is balanced, suggesting broad product appeal.

---

### 💰 Spending Behaviour

- Most customers make moderate purchases.
- A small segment of customers contributes significantly to overall revenue.
- Premium customers present opportunities for loyalty and retention programs.

---

### 🛒 Product Categories

- Certain categories consistently outperform others in terms of revenue.
- Inventory planning should prioritize high-performing categories.
- Low-performing categories may benefit from targeted promotions.

---

### ⭐ Customer Satisfaction

- Review ratings remain relatively consistent across product categories.
- Purchase amount is influenced by multiple factors beyond customer ratings.

---

### 🚚 Shipping Analysis

- Customers exhibit clear preferences for selected shipping methods.
- Optimizing popular shipping options can improve customer satisfaction while reducing logistics costs.

---

### 📅 Seasonal Trends

- Purchasing behavior varies across seasons.
- Seasonal demand should guide inventory planning and promotional campaigns.

---

### 🌍 Geographic Analysis

- Revenue differs significantly across locations.
- High-performing regions deserve increased marketing investment.

---

# 📌 Business Recommendations

- Implement loyalty programs for premium customers.
- Increase inventory for top-selling categories.
- Personalize marketing campaigns based on customer demographics.
- Optimize shipping methods according to customer preferences.
- Launch promotional campaigns before peak shopping seasons.
- Focus marketing efforts on high-revenue regions.
